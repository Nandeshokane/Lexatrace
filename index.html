Process

How LexaTrace Works

A five-step pipeline from upload to actionable report, powered by cutting-edge NLP.

1

Upload Your Content

Upload documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT), source code (ZIP), images (PNG/JPG), or audio files. We support files up to 15MB with automatic format detection and MIME validation.

2

Extract & Chunk

Our pipeline extracts text using pdfminer, python-docx, and pygments. Content is split into 512-token windows with 50-token overlap for thorough coverage.

3

Generate Embeddings

Each chunk is encoded with sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) into semantic vector space. Images are processed through CLIP (ViT-B/32) for visual similarity.

4

Search & Match

Embeddings are queried against our Elasticsearch KNN index and patent databases (USPTO, EPO, WIPO). Cosine similarity scoring with calibrated thresholds identifies matches.

5

Report & Recommend

A comprehensive risk report is generated with overall scores, match details, jurisdiction mapping (US/EU/Global), legal references, and royalty-free alternatives. Export as PDF instantly.
